Our Branding Process
01

Discovery & Brand Brief

We deep-dive into your business, audience, competitors, and goals. We learn your personality before we design a pixel.

02

Concept Development

We develop 2–3 distinct logo directions, each with rationale and mood. You choose the direction that feels right.

03

Visual System Build

We expand the chosen direction into a full identity — colour palette, typography, icons, and brand patterns.

04

Brand Guidelines Document

A complete brand guide covering every use case — web, print, social, and beyond — so your brand stays consistent forever.

05

Handover & Rollout

All files in every format (SVG, PNG, PDF, EPS). We can also apply the new brand to your website, social profiles, and print materials.
